Fluid, Lightweight & Responsive

My old graphics design professor once said: "Good design is something that stands out as impressive. Great design is something that enhances the user experience without ever drawing attention to itself." This site features subtle animations on the menus that you might not even notice, but really complete the feeling of a well-made website.

The site uses the Bootstrap CSS framework. It is completely adaptive to all screen sizes. I alse worked directly with the designer to ensure the best user experience on even the smallest of phones! (Mainly because my iPhone is tiny!)

Finally, the site is amazingly SEO ready - everything about the code is lightweight and streamlined. My custom-code solution even removed some of the painful bloat that Wordpress likes to add, but that most people never need!
